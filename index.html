Kathleen McNamara

Born February 16th, 1993, Kathleen McNamara has always been an artist. After discovering her love for performance art, She has taken on leadership rolls and performance oppotunities all along the east coast. Her specialty is fire and led performance art utilizing hoops, a dragon staff, a contact sword, and numerous other props.

In her professional life, Kathleen has a degree of applied science in Occupational Therapy Assisting. Her most recent pursuit is web development in a course at University of North Carolina
